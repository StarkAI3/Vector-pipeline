DMA Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What is the DMA?
A: The Directorate of Municipal Administration (DMA) is the state-level agency responsible for overseeing and supporting the functioning of municipal corporations and councils across the state. It provides policy guidance, technical assistance, and monitors the performance of urban local bodies.

Q: How can I pay my property tax online?
A: You can pay property tax online by visiting your municipal corporation's official website, navigating to the 'Online Services' section, selecting 'Property Tax Payment', entering your property ID, and completing the payment through net banking, debit card, or credit card. You will receive an instant digital receipt.

Q: What documents are required for building plan approval?
A: For building plan approval, you need to submit: 1) Property ownership documents (sale deed or property card), 2) Survey layout and plot plan, 3) Architectural drawings with floor plans, elevations, and sections, 4) Structural stability certificate from a licensed engineer, 5) NOC from the fire department (for buildings over 15 meters), and 6) Environmental clearance (if applicable). All documents should be submitted in both physical and digital formats.

Q: How long does it take to get a birth certificate?
A: Birth certificates can be obtained within 7 working days if the birth was registered within 21 days of the event. For delayed registrations (after 21 days but within one year), it takes 15 working days. For very late registrations (after one year), the process may take 30 days as it requires additional verification and approval from senior officials.

Question: What is the procedure for filing a complaint about municipal services?
Answer: You can file complaints through multiple channels: 1) Online through the municipal corporation website's grievance portal, 2) Call the 24/7 helpline number (1800-XXX-XXXX), 3) Visit the municipal office and fill out a complaint form, 4) Use the mobile app to submit complaints with photos, or 5) Email the concerned department. All complaints are assigned a unique tracking number and must be resolved within 15 working days.

Q: How can I get a trade license for my business?
A: To obtain a trade license, follow these steps: 1) Visit the municipal corporation website and fill out the online application form, 2) Upload required documents including proof of premises (rent agreement or ownership), ID proof, and business registration certificate, 3) Pay the application fee online (varies based on business type), 4) Schedule an inspection by municipal officials, 5) After successful inspection, the license will be issued within 10 working days. The license is valid for one year and must be renewed annually.

Q: मला माझा जन्म दाखला कसा मिळेल?
A: जन्म दाखला मिळवण्यासाठी आपल्या नगर निगमच्या नोंदणी विभागाला भेट द्या किंवा ऑनलाइन पोर्टलवर अर्ज करा. आपल्याला जन्माची नोंदणी क्रमांक, पालकांचे ओळखपत्र आणि जन्म दाखला देणार्‍या रुग्णालयाचे प्रमाणपत्र लागेल. नोंदणीनंतर ७ कामकाजाच्या दिवसांत दाखला मिळेल.

Question: What are the timings for municipal offices?
Answer: Most municipal offices operate from Monday to Friday, 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM, with a lunch break from 1:30 PM to 2:00 PM. Some offices also open on Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM. However, online services are available 24/7. Emergency services like water supply repairs and sanitation issues can be reported round the clock through the helpline.

Q: How do I check my water bill?
A: You can check your water bill by: 1) Visiting the municipal website and entering your consumer number, 2) Using the mobile app with your registered mobile number, 3) Calling the customer care helpline and providing your consumer number, or 4) Visiting the nearest municipal office with your consumer number. Bills are generated every two months and can be paid online or at authorized collection centers.

Q: What is the procedure for getting a new water connection?
A: For a new water connection: 1) Submit an online application on the municipal website, 2) Upload property documents, ownership proof, and building plan approval, 3) Pay the application and security deposit fee, 4) A technical team will conduct a site survey within 10 days, 5) After survey approval, the connection will be installed within 30 days, and 6) You'll receive a consumer number and welcome kit with payment details.

Q: मालमत्ता कर माफीची योजना कधी उपलब्ध आहे?
A: मालमत्ता कर माफीची योजना सामान्यतः वर्षातून दोनदा, मार्च आणि सप्टेंबर महिन्यात उपलब्ध असते. या योजनेत मागील थकबाकी करांवरील दंड आणि व्याज माफ केले जाते. अचूक तारखा आणि अटींसाठी नगर निगमच्या वेबसाइट किंवा स्थानिक कार्यालयाशी संपर्क साधा.

Q: How can I report illegal construction in my area?
A: To report illegal construction: 1) Take photographs and videos as evidence, 2) Note the exact location with landmarks, 3) File a complaint through the municipal website's enforcement section, 4) Call the anti-encroachment helpline, or 5) Email the town planning department with details and evidence. All complaints are investigated within 7 days, and appropriate action is taken based on findings.
